Remove a category from the knowledge base.

Arguments: $ARGUMENTS
(Provide the category name or slug to remove, e.g. "Agents" or "agents")

Steps:
1. Call `kb_workspace_init` to get the workspace.
2. List the current categories from the workspace so you can match $ARGUMENTS to the correct category_slug.
3. Confirm with the user which category will be deleted and warn that ALL its content (sections, subcategories, wiki files) will be permanently removed.
4. Once confirmed, call `kb_category_manage` with action `remove_category` and the matched category_slug.
5. Confirm the category has been removed.
