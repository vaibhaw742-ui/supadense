Four verbs.
One compounding loop.

Most tools store. Supadense thinks alongside you — personalising what you read, threading it into your graph, surfacing what you almost forgot, and getting denser every day you use it.
01 · capture

Personalised.
Proactive.

Drop a paper, paste a thread, hit ⌘+shift+S on any tab. Supadense pulls claims, code, and citations — biased by what you already keep.

02 · connect

It threads into
what you already know.

Every fragment auto-links to prior reading — papers, half-thoughts, code you've shipped. No tagging. The graph compounds on every capture.

03 · surface

Notes that
make you learn back.

Spaced reviews. Gap calls. "you wondered about this in June" nudges. Quiet, on cadence — not another inbox.

Each session makes the next denser. Can't be cloned by feature parity — only by years of your own data. The longer you use it, the more it sounds like past-you.
