[**Supadense**](/) is your agentic second brain for engineers — it helps you upskill, learn, and stay in the top 1%.

It's available as a web app that you access through your browser.

---

## Getting Started

Supadense is a hosted web application. To get started:

1. **Sign up** — head to the app and request early access.
2. **Set up your Knowledge Base** — the agent will walk you through a quick onboarding to understand your goals and experience level.
3. **Add resources** — paste links to articles, YouTube videos, PDFs, or any learning material you want to retain.

---

## Knowledge Base

Your KB is the core of Supadense. It's a structured, AI-powered repository of everything you've learned.

### Categories

When you onboard, you define 2–6 knowledge categories (e.g. `agents`, `rag`, `llm inference`). Each category gets its own wiki page with:

- Key concepts
- Sources and references
- Images and diagrams
- Subcategories for deeper dives

### Adding Resources

You can add a resource at any time by sharing a URL or content with the agent. Supadense will:

1. Extract the key ideas
2. Map them to your knowledge categories
3. Update your wiki page automatically

---

## Wiki

Every knowledge category gets a dedicated wiki page at `/wiki`. You can:

- Browse concepts by section tab
- View all sources and images in the right panel
- Navigate between categories and subcategories from the sidebar

---

## Chat

The chat interface is your primary way to interact with Supadense. You can:

- Ask questions — answers come exclusively from your KB
- Add new resources — paste a URL and the agent processes it
- Explore connections — ask how concepts relate to each other

:::tip
Use `kb_retrieve` under the hood to search your KB. The agent will never answer from general training knowledge — only from what you've added.
:::

---

## Roadmaps

Structured learning paths live under `/wiki/roadmap`. Each roadmap gives you a curated sequence of topics to work through based on your goals and depth level.

---

## Customize

To make Supadense yours:

- **Adjust depth** — set each category to `deep`, `working`, or `awareness`
- **Add trusted sources** — tell the agent which platforms you prefer (e.g. Andrej Karpathy, Arxiv, Hugging Face)
- **Edit your goals** — update your onboarding profile at any time by asking the agent
