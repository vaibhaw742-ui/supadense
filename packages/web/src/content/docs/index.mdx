[**Supadense**](/) is an AI-powered knowledge base for engineers. You feed it learning resources — articles, YouTube videos, tweets, papers, docs — and it extracts the key ideas, organizes them into your personal wiki, and lets you query that knowledge through a chat interface.

Unlike a general AI assistant, Supadense only answers from what **you** have added. Every answer is grounded in your KB.

---

## How it works

1. **Onboard** — define your knowledge goals and categories (e.g. `agents`, `rag`, `llm-inference`)
2. **Ingest** — paste links to anything you want to learn from; the AI curator extracts and organizes it
3. **Browse** — your wiki at `/wiki` reflects everything you've learned, structured by category
4. **Ask** — chat with the agent and get answers drawn exclusively from your KB

---

## Core concepts

| Concept | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Workspace** | Your KB root — one workspace per project or learning goal |
| **Category** | A top-level knowledge domain (e.g. `agents`, `rag`) |
| **Section** | A named part of a category page (e.g. `key-concepts`, `use-cases`) |
| **Resource** | A URL or piece of content you've added to the KB |
| **Placement** | Where a resource's extracted content lands in the wiki |
| **Group** | A cluster of related concepts across resources in a section |
